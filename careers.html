A comparison of our career paths

introduction

Within our team there is a huge contrast in our career aspirations from supporting roles in ICT service desk operations and data science to leading roles in marketing, managing and executive positions. There is a lot that differentiates these roles however they all share a common link in information communication technology. An in depth analysis of these roles and the need for them in the industry can be found in our Industry Data Report
