Our team name

In thinking about a name for our team, we drew inspiration from prominent pioneers in information technology. One of our members brought their personal favourite, Grace Hopper, back to the team. Hopper was a real admiral in the US Navy, was one of the first programmers on the Harvard Mark I computer, invented one of the first linkers, and popularised the concept of machine-independent programming languages, leading to the development of COBOL. As an extremely accomplished, entrepreneurial, and forthright person, she is a great figure to draw inspiration from in our activities.

To learn more about Hopper, go to https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grace_Hopper

The Right Blend

Having a window in a room lets you see what is going on outside, in the same way personality tests let you see what is going on inside another person. Prior knowledge of what to expect from another person can help greatly in effective teamwork as everyone deals with stressors differently. Knowing what position each member will perform their best in and what they require to thrive will aid the team to succeed. When making wine you select different grapes to add different characteristics to the final blend, the individual Gracehoppers all have special characteristics and when blended form a full-bodied strong brew with a smooth sharp flavour.

“When a flower doesn’t bloom, you fix the environment in which it grows, not the flower.”
― Alexander Den Heijer
