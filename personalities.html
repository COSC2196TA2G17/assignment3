The Right Blend

Having a window in a room lets you see what is going on outside, in the same way personality tests let you see what is going on inside another person. Prior knowledge of what to expect from another person can help greatly in effective teamwork as everyone deals with stressors differently. Knowing what position each member will perform their best in and what they require to thrive will aid the team to succeed. When making wine you select different grapes to add different characteristics to the final blend, the individual Gracehoppers all have special characteristics and when blended form a full-bodied strong brew with a smooth sharp flavour.
