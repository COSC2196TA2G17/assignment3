Skills and Jobs

Key roles to take the project forward

Below are a series of positions required to take SMATRA forward to becoming a commercialised application and programme.

Chief Executive Officer
Product Delivery Manager
Public Relations and Marketing Specialist
Full Stack Developer

Chief Executive Officer

Summary

As Chief Executive Officer of the exciting Melbourne based start-up, SMATRA, you will be managing an amazing team of individuals, linking transportation corporations with rewards programme vendors, to get our cities moving and help solve transit congestion.

Responsibilities

As CEO of SMATRA, responsibilities will vary depending on the strategic goals and challenges of the company, some of the day to day responsibilities include:

  • High level strategic goal planning for the SMATRA programme and company
  • Managing and directing the organisation to achieve its strategic objectives
  • Operational leadership of the SMATRA programme and company
  • Building strategic corporate relationships with transportation companies and rewards programme vendors
  • Answering to the SMATRA board
  • Directing and motivating the SMATRA team

Skills

We are looking for an experienced leader that can drive the SMATRA team and programme forward, some of the skills necessary to perform this role will include:

  • The ability to effectively manage financial resources
  • Aligning and improving organisational systems and resources to strategy
  • Articulate, motivate and correspond effectively with all facets of the business and partners
  • The ability to build strategic partnerships, particularly in the transportation and corporate rewards spaces
  • A qualification as an MBA, CA or CPA would be an advantage

Product Delivery Manager

Summary

As Product Delivery Manager of the exciting Melbourne based start-up, SMATRA, you will be managing an amazing delivery team, building applications that get our cities moving and help solve transit congestion.

Responsibilities

As a Product Delivery Manager, you will be translating the CEO’s vision into the SMATRA’s product roadmap and be responsible for its delivery, some of the responsibilities of the role include:

  • Work closely with the CEO to manage the product backlog; considering business objectives, partner and client considerations, dependencies, and technical effort required
  • Communicating functional, development and programming requirements to a level required by the delivery team. This may include but is not limited to screen mock-ups, details of calculations, user journeys and acceptance criteria
  • Understand and provide mentoring on appropriate Agile delivery frameworks
  • Run diagnostics on the Agile maturity of the team, and refine processes to enhance delivery cadence
  • Working with the CEO and delivery team to formulating and direct information technology strategies, policies, and plans

Skills

We are looking for an experienced Product Delivery Manager that can drive the SMATRA delivery team, some of the skills necessary to perform this role will include:

  • In-depth knowledge of Agile process and principles
  • Experience in leading delivery teams
  • Clear and concise verbal and written communication skills
  • Knowledge of common product management, wire-framing, testing and analytics tools (Jira, Google Analytics, Draw.IO, CloudWatch, DataDog, Amazon Pinpoint, etc.)
  • Technical background/UI design and implementation experience would be beneficial

Public Relations and Marketing Specialist

Summary

As Public Relations and Marketing Specialist of the exciting Melbourne based start-up, SMATRA, you will be responsible for developing our client base and managing all public communications, to get our cities moving and help solve transit congestion.

Responsibilities

As a Public Relations and Marketing Specialist, you will be translating the CEO’s vision into the SMATRA’s marketing and communications strategy and responsible for its execution, some of the responsibilities of the role include:

  • Will drive brand awareness and develop the marketing strategy for SMATRA
  • SMATRA brand management and corporate identity including all marketing assets
  • Analyse and interpret data to understand customer behaviour and define customer segments
  • Manage implementation and execution of key marketing initiatives, internal and external events, and promotional campaigns
  • Advise and support the CEO in developing relationships with new and existing strategic partners

Skills

We are looking for an experienced Public Relations and Marketing Specialist that can drive the SMATRA marketing and communications strategy, some of the skills necessary to perform this role will include:

  • Demonstrated experience in leading marketing strategy and business development
  • Extensive experience in digital marketing and communications, particularly in application promotion
  • Tertiary qualifications in Marketing, Business Studies, Communications, or related field
  • Ability to effectively build strong relationships with corporate partners
  • Knowledge of common product management and analytics tools (Jira, Google Analytics, DataDog, etc.)

Full Stack Developer

Summary

As a Full Stack Developer of the exciting Melbourne based start-up, SMATRA, you will be working in an exciting team, developing applications to get our cities moving and help solve transit congestion.

Responsibilities

As a Full Stack Developer, you will be developing the Product Delivery Manager delivery items into the SMATRA applications and be responsible for the applications delivery, maintenance, and operation, some of the responsibilities of the role include:

  • Developing both applications and websites using a mixture of creativity and software programming
  • Designing, coding, securing, testing, deploying, and operating using modern DevSecOps processes
  • Analysing user stories and the product roadmap, and designing appropriate solutions to suit requirements
  • Work with users in an agile development environment to collaborate, understand requirements, elicit feedback, and refine the application and processes
  • Provide input into the viability, estimation and delivery of user stories and technical tasks

Skills

We are looking for an experienced Full Stack Developer that can develop, maintain, and operate the SMATRA suite of applications, some of the skills necessary to perform this role will include:

  • Advanced knowledge of and experience with Node.js, React and React Native
  • Experience with HTML, CSS & JavaScript (ES6)
  • Experience with developing and integrating GraphQL
  • Experience with DynamoDB or equivalent NoSQL databases
  • Experience working with, deploying to, and monitoring AWS infrastructure
  • Tertiary qualifications in an IT related field e.g. Bachelor of Information Technology
  • Knowledge of common product management, wire-framing, testing and analytics tools (Jira, Google Analytics, Draw.IO, CloudWatch, DataDog, Amazon Pinpoint, etc.)
